Future-Genhack Competition.

Project Overview

Future-Genhack Competition is competition that resembles a hackathon in which the participant were given 48 hours to complete a mobile app using Adobe XD as a base. After completing the task, participants are then required to present their application to determine the winner

The project were assign to us with the time span of 48 hours. The project is quite difficult as it is the first time our group learning Adobe XD as our silibus is not yet been tought during that year. We learned to use Adobe XD in a very limited time and knowledge as we also need to record a video and present what is the function of our application

After presenting towards the judges, our group were given 4th Place out of 6 group in which a great achievment as our group consists of Year 1 and compete with Year 2 and 3 Students of Computer Science
